# Digital Diachrony: Professional Development Program
### National and Kapodistrian University of Athens (KEDIVIM)

**Scientific Director:** Prof. Nikolaos Lavidas  
**Department:** English Language and Literature

## Program Overview
"Digital Tools for the Study of Language Evolution" is a specialized professional development program (8 months / 12 ECTS) designed to bridge the gap between traditional philology and modern computational linguistics.

**Key Features:**
- **Open Access:** All tools and methodologies are based on open science principles.
- **Interdisciplinary:** Combines diachronic linguistics with Python/R and NLP.
- **Accredited:** Certificate of Specialized Training (Level 6 EQF) from KEDIVIM NKUA.
